Alter Ego

Alter Ego is a studio album by Thai artist Lisa, a member of the K-pop group Blackpink. In this album, the artist explores five alter egos, respectively named Roxi, Sunni, Kiki, Speedi, and Vixi.

Roxi

I’m a Rockstar, baby... Music is all around me because it is me. I escape into a world that is electrifying.

Sunni

Bonjour, I’m Sunni. The beach is my sanctuary, where the golden rays warm my skin.

Kiki

I live for the thrill—whether it’s the perfect playlist or a late-night adventure.

Speedi

Built for the rush, wired for the win. I’ll give you a new meaning to fast fashion.

Vixi

I’m Vixi— the one they love to call the “Villain”. My lashes came to slay.
